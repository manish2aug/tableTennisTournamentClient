Points balance calculation
Let's say player A, plays against player B,C and D and the scores look like the following

A vs B: 11-6, 11-3 (Winner: A)
A vs C: 6-11, 9-11 (Winner: C)
A vs D: 11-5, 10-12, 11-9 (Winner: A)

Points earned by player A
      = ((total points gained by A) - (total points gained opponents)) / total no of games played by A
      = ( (11 + 11 + 6 + 9 + 11 + 10 + 11) - (6 + 3 + 11 + 11 + 5 + 12 + 9))/7
      = (69 - 57)/7
      = 1.714
